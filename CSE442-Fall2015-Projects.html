DailyFantasySports - DFS

Daily Fantasy Sports is a fast-growing, multi-billion dollar industry. Building successful lineups can be lucrative, but difficult and time consuming. Our team plans to use all of our sports, DFS, and programming knowledge to automate this process, while still achieving good results. We are starting with Mixed Martial Arts, and plan to move into other sports later such as Football, Golf, and Nascar as time allows. Our initial projection model will focus on generating point projections based on vegas odds and the DraftKings scoring system, but we have many other ideas to improve our projections later. We intend to acquire the pricing spreadsheet from the DFS website, scrape the vegas odds for various outcomes, and calculate good lineups based on the data from the spreadsheet.
